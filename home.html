Who we are

Joy Aid Foundation is a non-governmental organization with a focus on ensuring sustainable and inclusive development by reaching out to the greater masses of disadvantaged individuals and communities, through targeted provision of education, technology and environmental sustainability to establish sustainable talents amongst the youth in Africa and beyond.



Our Vision

To become ambassadors of the green revolution by supporting green growth leading to poverty reduction and to promote the mantle of leadership by eliminating gender bias and providing entrepreneurial skill.

Our Mission

Creating prosperity by reaching out to the greater masses of disadvantaged people,leadership, environmental care and honing talents and skill-sets among the youth in Africa

Our Key Objectives:

  • Alleviating extreme poverty within communities across the country through innovative and practical solutions that reach the needs of the less privileged and vulnerable;
  • To promote good governance and leadership, and eliminate gender bias through the provision of entrepreneurial and value-addition skill development opportunities;
  • Sustain global partnerships that promote the cause of quality education, advance green growth and community development;
  • Promote volunteerism and advocacy to empower social enterprise development, and related matters.
